- Following work instructions

## üí° Scoring Guidelines

### 90-100% - Exceptional Match
- All required skills met
- Multiple preferred skills
- Relevant certifications
- Strong experience (exceeds minimum)
- Safety record mentioned
- **Action:** Schedule interview immediately, priority candidate

### 75-89% - Strong Match
- Most required skills met
- Some preferred skills
- Experience meets requirements
- Basic certifications
- **Action:** Recommended for interview

### 60-74% - Good Match
- Core required skills present
- Experience slightly below ideal
- Missing some certifications
- **Action:** Consider for interview, may need training

### 40-59% - Moderate Match
- Some required skills missing
- Limited experience
- Few/no certifications
- **Action:** May work for entry-level with training

### 0-39% - Poor Match
- Lacks essential skills
- No relevant experience
- Wrong industry background
- **Action:** Not recommended

## üîç Resume Red Flags (Lower Scores)

- **Job hopping** - Multiple short-term jobs (< 6 months)
- **Employment gaps** - Unexplained periods without work
- **No certifications** - For roles requiring forklift, welding, etc.
- **Wrong industry** - No manufacturing/production background
- **Overqualified** - College degree for entry-level labor position
- **Location issues** - Candidate too far from facility
- **Shift conflicts** - Can't work required shift

## ‚úÖ Resume Green Flags (Higher Scores)

- **Longevity** - 2+ years at previous employers
- **Perfect attendance** - Mentioned explicitly
- **Certifications** - OSHA, forklift, AWS, PLC training
- **Safety awards** - Zero incidents, safety recognition
- **Cross-training** - Multiple machines/skills
- **Promotions** - Advanced from operator to lead/supervisor
- **Shift flexibility** - Available for any shift
- **Bilingual** - English + Spanish common in manufacturing

## üéì Best Practices for Manufacturing Recruiting

1. **Certifications Matter** - Forklift, welding, OSHA are must-haves for many roles
2. **Attendance is King** - Look for mentions of perfect attendance or reliability
3. **Safety Record** - Zero incidents is a huge plus
4. **Shift Flexibility** - Candidates open to any shift are more valuable
5. **Physical Ability** - Ensure candidate mentions ability to meet physical demands
6. **Location** - Verify candidate is within reasonable commute distance
7. **Drug Testing** - Most manufacturing requires passing drug screen
8. **Background Check** - Verify candidate mentions passing background checks
9. **Fast Hiring** - Manufacturing moves fast, contact top candidates within 24-48 hours
10. **Skills Tests** - Consider weld tests, forklift tests, or machine setup tests
